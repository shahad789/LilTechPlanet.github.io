Vision

At LilTechPlanet, our vision is centered on the concept of dynamic and captivating education. Our goal is to offer children an accessible and enjoyable learning experience through a user-friendly interface. This interface will grant them access to a diverse spectrum of programming languages, ranging from Scratch for beginners to more advanced languages like Python and JavaScript. In addition, we are committed to providing a structured developmental path for children. We understand the importance of parental involvement and will ensure that parents have access to progress reports, allowing them to actively engage in their child's educational voyage.

In a PHP course, students learn how to embed PHP code within HTML to process forms, interact with databases, and perform a wide range of server-side tasks.
